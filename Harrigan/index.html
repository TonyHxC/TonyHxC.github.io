About

The folks at Harrigan Insurance have the expertise and interest to provide residents of the River Valley with first-rate service and products for all their insurance needs. They are highly motivated towards ongoing professional development and are keenly committed to making a meaningful contribution to our community.

History

History - One of the River Valley area’s oldest business establishments, providing uniterrupted service under the same ownership for more than 40 years. Harrigan insurance was founded in 1964 as a true “Mom-and-Pop” outfit in the Morrisdale home of Bruce M. Harrigan and his Wife, Mary. This entrepeneurial step taken after Bruce had spent some 11 years cutting his teeth while writing insurance policies for other companies.

Mission Statement

Harrigan Insurance Team aims to provide top-notch service. Having built a firm foundation in its past, Harrigan Insurance Agency Ltd., is not resting on its laurels. Today, the agency continues to be a vibrant part of the River Valley business community, committed to keeping up with the times while at the same time, maintaining the “human touch” established over the last four decades.
